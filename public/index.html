Biography

I am a PhD candidate at the Epidemiology Department of Erasmus MC. My research is focused in applying causal inference methods in cohort studies, specifically to emulate target trials for long-term exposures related to dementia and to understand the role of competing events in this setting.

I enjoy teaching epidemiology, R programing and to work with communities. This is why I help in the organization of R-Ladies Rotterdam, Reprohack and recently became an RStudio Certified Instructor.

I keep my heart close to home in La Paz, Bolivia.
